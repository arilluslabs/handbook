title: Our Values
description: The four values that guide every decision at Arillus — assessed in appraisals, probed in every interview.
---

These values are assessed in appraisals alongside level expectations, and probed in every interview loop.

## 1. Stay Curious

**Ask why, explore deeply, and never stop learning.**

We challenge assumptions, seek to understand the real problem, and remain open to better ideas — regardless of where they come from.

This includes intellectual honesty: saying "I don't know," investigating it, and changing our minds when the evidence changes.

## 2. Choose Simplicity

**Remove unnecessary complexity.**

We seek the simplest solution that genuinely solves the problem — not shortcuts, oversimplification, or cleverness for its own sake.

This applies to products, user experiences, technology, communication, processes, and decisions.

## 3. Own the Outcome

**Take responsibility for the result, not merely the assigned task.**

We do not stop at "my part is done." We raise blockers early, follow through, collaborate across boundaries, and stay engaged until the intended outcome is achieved.

## 4. Build Together

**The best outcomes emerge when different perspectives work as one team.**

We share context, ask for help early, give honest feedback, and treat product, design, engineering, QA, and delivery as partners — not separate departments handing work to one another.

No waiting silently. No "that's not my responsibility." No information trapped with one person.
